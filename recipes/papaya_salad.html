Papaya Salad

Description

Thum Mak Hoong as it's called in Laos is an essential dish for us at the table. Especially when eating chicken or steak.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Unripe Papaya
  • Fermented Fish Sauce (Pa Daek)
  • Salt
  • Lime
  • Fish Sauce
  • Chilli Pepper
  • MSG
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Clove of Garlic

Steps

  1. Using a pestle and mortar, add how many ever chilli peppers you would like.
  2. Crush the peppers and garlic for a bit and then add 1/2 a tablespoon of fermented fish sauce.
  3. Add a pinch or pinches of salt, a 1/4 teaspoon of MSG, squeeze some lime in there.
  4. Add a table spoon of fish sauce, and then and continue grinding it until they are all combined.
  5. After that put in the shredded papaya and mix. You can add the cherry tomatoes before to mash them as well or after it's a preference
  6. Adjust the seasoning after if it isn't to your liking and then enjoy.
